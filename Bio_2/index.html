About

Hayden Wilson is a first year Lethbridge college student in the multimedia program focused mainly on graphic design. Hayden won the art award for his high school for three years in a row. When Hayden goes to relax you can probably find him either with his friends or with his family. Whether he is just playing videogames or out and about with his friends he is always guaranteed to enjoy his time.
