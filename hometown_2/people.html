Coaldale is a very community-driven town, and we are always welcoming to new people whether it's just someone passing through or a new resident. The politicians and town council care and listen to the citizens such as the revitalization of the Coaldale skate park and the constant work on the road quality. There are also constantly people donating to the food bank just sheerly out the kindness of their heart. Coaldale is also full of people of varying cultures and faiths. Coaldale’s population is made up of Caucasian, Asian, Black and Indigenous citizens.

The churches that are present are Catholic, Anglican, Mennonite, Mormon and Protestant However there are several people that are different faiths such as Buddhist and Agnostic. People from Coaldale are typically helpful and kind if you ask for some directions when you’re passing through. Nine times out of ten you will be greeted with a person smiling giving you descriptive directions.
