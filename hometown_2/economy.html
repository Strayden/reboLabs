The major businesses in Coaldale are located in the industrial park with companies building commercial vehicles that ship internationally. There also is lots of farmland that is used mainly to grow grain and corn. We also have the McCain plant and the Whole Leaf Lettuce greenhouse just outside of Coaldale going west on highway three.

Coaldale also has its own family-run Grocery store that was built from the ground up in 1980 and has been an amazing source of work experience for many of Coaldale’s youth.We also have an ice skating and curling rink in the winter and a swimming pool next to it during the summer. There is also a handful of small shops down Coaldale’s main street. We also have one hotel and two motels. There is also plenty of construction jobs with people either renovating their houses or just building new residences as the town is constantly expanding.
