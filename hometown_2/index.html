Welcome to The Gem of the West more commonly known as Coaldale. We are a decent- sized town, that’s just a 17 Kilometers east from Lethbridge, with a population just under 9,000. Coaldale is a town that is primary a residential town where a family may want to raise their children. We also have multiple private and public schools and lots of business opportunities. We also have a variety of different green, well-kept parks scattered throughout the town most of which have ponds or small bodies of water. We also have biking and walking paths that are a great way to spend some time in the evenings and on weekends.

If you aren’t looking to stay and just want to pass by, come visit the Birds of Prey Center where you can see hawks and bald eagles on their Hawk Walk.
